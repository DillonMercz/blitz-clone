Google Calendar Blitzit integration



Blitzit supports an integration with Google Calendar.

Integrate your calendar by opening the integrations pop-over and selecting the Google Calendar option.

Go through the setup process and login via the required Gmail account to import events (e.g. meetings) to any Blitzit task list.

Blitzit automatically syncs your Google Calendar every 24hrs, but you can also manually sync your events yourself by simply clicking the Sync button on the Google Calendar Integration Popover found inside your list.


Troubleshooting

If you don't see your events, try using the Refresh option found in the Google Integrations Popover, followed by clicking the Manual Sync button.
